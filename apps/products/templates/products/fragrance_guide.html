Fragrance Guide

Understand Your Scent

Learn about fragrance families, notes, and how to choose the perfect scent for every occasion.

Fragrance Families

Citrus

Fresh, zesty notes like lemon, bergamot, and orange. Energetic and uplifting.

Floral

Romantic blossoms like rose, jasmine, and lavender. Feminine and timeless.

Woody

Warm, earthy scents like sandalwood, cedar, and oud. Grounding and sophisticated.

Oriental

Rich, exotic notes like amber, vanilla, and spices. Warm and sensual.

Fresh

Clean, aquatic, and green notes. Crisp and invigorating.

Fragrance Notes Pyramid

Every fragrance unfolds in three layers that reveal themselves over time:

T

Top Notes

First impression, lasts 15–30 minutes. Light and volatile molecules.

M

Middle Notes

The heart of the fragrance, lasts 3–5 hours. Full-bodied and defining.

B

Base Notes

The foundation, lasts 6–24 hours. Deep, rich, and lingering.

How to Choose Your Fragrance

1

Know Your Occasion

Light, fresh scents for daytime and work; deeper, richer fragrances for evenings and special events.

2

Consider the Season

Fresh citrus and greens for summer; warm woody and oriental notes for winter.

3

Test Before You Invest

Fragrances smell different on everyone. Allow the scent to develop on your skin before deciding.

4

Trust Your Instinct

The best fragrance is the one that makes you feel confident and happy. Wear what you love.
